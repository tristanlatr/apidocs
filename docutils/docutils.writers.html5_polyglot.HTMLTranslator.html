class HTMLTranslator(writers._html_base.HTMLTranslator):

View In Hierarchy

This writer generates polyglot markup: HTML5 that is also valid XML.

Safe subclassing: when overriding, treat visit_* and depart_* methods as a unit to prevent breaks due to internal changes. See the docstring of docutils.writers._html_base.HTMLTranslator for details and examples.
